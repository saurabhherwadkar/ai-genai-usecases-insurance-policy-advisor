HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: HO-2024-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Expiration Date: December 31, 2024

SECTION 1: PROPERTY COVERAGE

Coverage A - Dwelling
This policy covers the dwelling on the residence premises, including structures attached to the dwelling. The dwelling coverage limit is $350,000.

Coverage includes:
- Physical damage to the structure from covered perils
- Built-in appliances such as furnaces, water heaters, and central air conditioning
- Permanently installed fixtures like plumbing, electrical wiring, and carpeting
- Attached structures such as garages and decks

Coverage B - Other Structures
Structures on the residence premises that are separated from the dwelling by clear space are covered up to 10% of the Coverage A limit ($35,000). This includes:
- Detached garages
- Storage sheds
- Fences and retaining walls
- Swimming pools

Coverage C - Personal Property
Personal belongings are covered up to 50% of the Coverage A limit ($175,000). This includes:
- Furniture and household items
- Electronics and appliances
- Clothing and personal items
- Sports equipment

Special Limits on Personal Property:
- Money, coins, and precious metals: $200
- Securities, deeds, and manuscripts: $1,500
- Watercraft and trailers: $1,500
- Jewelry, watches, and furs: $1,500
- Firearms: $2,500
- Silverware and goldware: $2,500
- Business property on premises: $2,500
- Business property off premises: $500

Coverage D - Loss of Use
If a covered loss makes the residence premises uninhabitable, this policy covers:
- Additional living expenses up to 20% of Coverage A ($70,000)
- Fair rental value if part of the premises is rented
- Civil authority prohibitions (up to 2 weeks)

SECTION 2: COVERED PERILS

The following perils are covered under this policy:
1. Fire or lightning
2. Windstorm or hail
3. Explosion
4. Riot or civil commotion
5. Aircraft damage
6. Vehicle damage
7. Smoke damage
8. Vandalism or malicious mischief
9. Theft
10. Falling objects
11. Weight of ice, snow, or sleet
12. Accidental discharge of water or steam
13. Sudden tearing of heating/cooling systems
14. Freezing of plumbing, heating, and air conditioning
15. Sudden damage from artificially generated electrical current
16. Volcanic eruption

SECTION 3: EXCLUSIONS

This policy does NOT cover loss or damage caused by:
- Flood or surface water (separate flood insurance required)
- Earthquake or earth movement
- War or nuclear hazard
- Intentional loss caused by the insured
- Neglect or failure to protect property after a loss
- Power failure originating away from the premises
- Normal wear and tear or deterioration
- Mechanical breakdown
- Smog, rust, or corrosion
- Mold, fungus, or wet rot (unless resulting from a covered peril)
- Insects, rodents, or vermin
- Birds, domestic animals, or livestock owned by the insured
- Settling, cracking, or bulging of foundations
- Government action or ordinance (except as provided in Coverage D)

SECTION 4: LIABILITY COVERAGE

Coverage E - Personal Liability
This policy provides personal liability coverage up to $100,000 per occurrence for:
- Bodily injury to others on the premises
- Property damage caused by the insured or household members
- Liability arising from personal activities away from home

Coverage F - Medical Payments to Others
Medical payments coverage of $1,000 per person for:
- Medical expenses of guests injured on the premises
- Medical expenses from activities of the insured away from premises
- Does NOT apply to the insured or regular household members

SECTION 5: DEDUCTIBLES AND CONDITIONS

Deductible: $1,000 per occurrence (applies to property claims only)
Wind/Hail Deductible: 2% of Coverage A limit

Conditions:
- Premium must be paid on or before the due date
- Insured must notify the company promptly of any loss
- Insured must protect property from further damage after a loss
- Insured must cooperate with the company during claims investigation
- Proof of loss must be filed within 60 days of request
- No action may be brought against the company unless policy conditions are met

SECTION 6: DISCOUNTS AND ENDORSEMENTS

Available Discounts:
- Security system discount: 5%
- Fire alarm discount: 3%
- Multi-policy discount: 10%
- Claims-free discount: 15%
- New home discount (less than 5 years old): 8%

Optional Endorsements:
- Scheduled personal property (jewelry, art, collectibles)
- Water backup and sump overflow coverage
- Identity theft expense coverage
- Home business coverage
- Equipment breakdown coverage
