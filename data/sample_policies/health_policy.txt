HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: HE-2024-003
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Expiration Date: December 31, 2024
Plan Type: Preferred Provider Organization (PPO)

SECTION 1: ELIGIBILITY AND ENROLLMENT

Eligible Members:
- Named insured (primary policyholder)
- Legally married spouse or domestic partner
- Dependent children up to age 26
- Disabled dependents regardless of age (with documentation)

Enrollment Periods:
- Open enrollment: November 1 - December 15 annually
- Special enrollment: Within 60 days of qualifying life event
- Qualifying life events: marriage, birth, adoption, loss of other coverage, relocation

SECTION 2: PREMIUM AND COST-SHARING

Monthly Premium: $450 (individual) / $1,200 (family)
Annual Deductible: $2,000 (individual) / $4,000 (family)
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $8,000 (individual) / $16,000 (family)

Cost-Sharing Structure:
- Preventive care: $0 (no deductible applies)
- Primary care visit: $30 copay
- Specialist visit: $60 copay
- Urgent care: $75 copay
- Emergency room: $300 copay (waived if admitted)
- Generic prescriptions: $15 copay
- Preferred brand prescriptions: $40 copay
- Non-preferred brand prescriptions: $80 copay
- Specialty prescriptions: 20% coinsurance up to $250/month

After Deductible:
- In-network services: Plan pays 80%, member pays 20%
- Out-of-network services: Plan pays 60%, member pays 40%

SECTION 3: COVERED SERVICES

Preventive Care (no cost-sharing):
- Annual physical examination
- Well-child visits and immunizations
- Cancer screenings (mammogram, colonoscopy, pap smear)
- Cardiovascular screenings
- Diabetes screening
- Depression screening
- Flu shots and preventive vaccines
- Contraceptive methods and counseling

Hospital Services:
- Inpatient hospital care (semi-private room)
- Outpatient surgery and procedures
- Emergency room services
- Intensive care unit
- Maternity and newborn care
- Skilled nursing facility (up to 60 days per benefit period)

Medical Services:
- Primary care physician visits
- Specialist consultations
- Diagnostic testing (lab work, X-rays, MRI, CT scans)
- Physical therapy (up to 30 visits per year)
- Occupational therapy (up to 30 visits per year)
- Speech therapy (up to 20 visits per year)
- Chiropractic care (up to 20 visits per year)
- Durable medical equipment
- Home health care (up to 100 visits per year)
- Hospice care

Mental Health and Substance Abuse:
- Outpatient mental health visits: $30 copay
- Inpatient mental health: Same as hospital services
- Substance abuse treatment (inpatient and outpatient)
- Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) for autism
- Crisis intervention services

Prescription Drug Coverage:
- Tier 1 (Generic): $15 copay
- Tier 2 (Preferred Brand): $40 copay
- Tier 3 (Non-Preferred Brand): $80 copay
- Tier 4 (Specialty): 20% coinsurance, max $250/month
- Mail-order: 90-day supply at 2.5x copay
- Formulary exceptions available with prior authorization

Vision Services:
- Annual eye exam: $20 copay
- Eyeglass frames: $150 allowance every 24 months
- Contact lens fitting: covered
- Laser eye surgery: not covered

Dental Services (basic):
- Preventive cleaning: 2 per year, $0 copay
- Basic restorative (fillings): 20% coinsurance
- Major restorative (crowns, bridges): 50% coinsurance
- Orthodontia: not covered under this plan
- Annual dental maximum: $1,500

SECTION 4: EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS

Services NOT covered by this policy:
- Cosmetic surgery or procedures (unless medically necessary for reconstruction)
- Experimental or investigational treatments
- Weight loss surgery (unless BMI > 40 or BMI > 35 with comorbidities)
- Infertility treatments beyond diagnostic testing
- Long-term custodial care
- Services received outside the United States (except emergency)
- Work-related injuries covered by workers' compensation
- Services not deemed medically necessary
- Over-the-counter medications
- Hearing aids (covered for children under 18 only)
- Private duty nursing
- Acupuncture (limited to 12 visits/year for chronic pain only)
- Dental implants
- Routine foot care (unless diabetic)

Prior Authorization Required For:
- Inpatient hospital admissions (except emergency)
- Outpatient surgery
- Advanced imaging (MRI, CT, PET scans)
- Specialty medications
- Durable medical equipment over $500
- Physical/occupational therapy beyond 12 visits
- Home health care
- Skilled nursing facility stays
- Mental health inpatient admissions

SECTION 5: NETWORK INFORMATION

In-Network Benefits:
- Access to over 500,000 providers nationwide
- No referral required for specialists
- Lower cost-sharing amounts
- No balance billing

Out-of-Network Benefits:
- Higher deductible applies ($4,000 individual / $8,000 family)
- Higher coinsurance (40% member responsibility)
- Balance billing may apply
- Out-of-network out-of-pocket maximum: $16,000 / $32,000

SECTION 6: CLAIMS AND APPEALS

Filing a Claim:
- In-network providers file claims directly
- Out-of-network: Submit claim within 90 days of service
- Include itemized bill with diagnosis and procedure codes
- Claims processed within 30 days of receipt

Appeals Process:
- Level 1: Internal review within 30 days
- Level 2: External independent review within 45 days
- Expedited review available for urgent situations (72 hours)
- Members may designate an authorized representative

SECTION 7: WELLNESS PROGRAMS

Included wellness benefits:
- Health risk assessment: Annual online questionnaire
- Wellness coaching: Up to 6 telephonic sessions per year
- Gym membership discount: $150 annual reimbursement
- Smoking cessation program: Covered at 100%
- Weight management program: $500 annual benefit
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 6 free counseling sessions
- Telemedicine visits: $0 copay for virtual primary care
