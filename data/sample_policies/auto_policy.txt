AUTOMOBILE INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: AU-2024-002
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Expiration Date: December 31, 2024

SECTION 1: LIABILITY COVERAGE

Part A - Bodily Injury Liability
Coverage Limits: $250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident
This coverage pays for bodily injury or death resulting from an accident for which the insured is legally responsible.

Covered situations include:
- Injuries to other drivers, passengers, or pedestrians
- Legal defense costs for covered claims
- Court-ordered judgments up to the policy limit
- Settlement payments approved by the company

Part B - Property Damage Liability
Coverage Limit: $100,000 per accident
This coverage pays for damage to another person's property caused by the insured vehicle.

Covered property includes:
- Other vehicles
- Buildings and structures
- Fences, mailboxes, and landscaping
- Personal property inside damaged vehicles

SECTION 2: MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE

Coverage Limit: $10,000 per person
Medical payments coverage pays for reasonable medical expenses within 3 years of the accident for:
- The named insured and family members
- Passengers in the insured vehicle
- Pedestrians struck by the insured vehicle

Covered expenses include:
- Hospital and surgical bills
- X-rays and diagnostic imaging
- Dental treatment for injured teeth
- Ambulance services
- Prosthetic devices
- Funeral expenses up to $5,000

SECTION 3: UNINSURED/UNDERINSURED MOTORIST COVERAGE

Part C - Uninsured Motorist Bodily Injury
Coverage Limits: $250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident
Covers bodily injury caused by:
- An uninsured driver at fault
- A hit-and-run driver
- A driver whose insurance company is insolvent

Part D - Underinsured Motorist Coverage
Applies when the at-fault driver's liability limits are less than the insured's damages.

SECTION 4: COLLISION COVERAGE

Deductible: $500
Collision coverage pays for damage to the insured vehicle caused by:
- Collision with another vehicle
- Collision with an object (tree, guardrail, pole)
- Single-vehicle rollover
- Damage caused in a parking lot

This coverage applies regardless of fault.

SECTION 5: COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE

Deductible: $250
Comprehensive coverage pays for damage to the insured vehicle from non-collision events:
- Theft or attempted theft
- Vandalism
- Fire or explosion
- Falling objects (trees, branches, debris)
- Natural disasters (hail, wind, flood)
- Animal collisions (deer, birds)
- Glass breakage
- Riots and civil disturbances
- Missile damage

SECTION 6: EXCLUSIONS

This policy does NOT cover:
- Vehicles used for commercial purposes (delivery, rideshare without endorsement)
- Racing, speed contests, or demolition events
- Intentional damage caused by the insured
- Wear and tear, mechanical breakdown, or tire damage (unless caused by collision)
- Custom equipment or modifications not declared to the insurer
- Vehicles not listed on the policy
- Drivers specifically excluded from the policy
- Damage from war, nuclear events, or radioactive contamination
- Loss to personal belongings inside the vehicle (covered by homeowners/renters policy)
- Vehicles used to carry persons or property for a fee
- Damage occurring while fleeing law enforcement

SECTION 7: ADDITIONAL COVERAGES

Rental Reimbursement: $50/day, maximum 30 days
- Covers rental car costs while the insured vehicle is being repaired from a covered loss

Towing and Labor: $100 per disablement
- Covers towing to the nearest qualified repair facility
- On-scene labor costs (tire change, battery jump, lockout)

Gap Coverage:
- Pays the difference between the vehicle's actual cash value and the remaining loan balance
- Applies only to total loss situations

New Car Replacement:
- For vehicles less than 1 year old with fewer than 15,000 miles
- Replaces totaled vehicle with a new one of same make and model

SECTION 8: PREMIUM FACTORS AND DISCOUNTS

Premium is determined by:
- Driver age, gender, and marital status
- Driving record (accidents, violations)
- Annual mileage
- Vehicle make, model, year, and safety features
- Garaging location (ZIP code)
- Credit-based insurance score
- Coverage selections and deductible amounts

Available Discounts:
- Multi-car discount: 15%
- Good driver (no accidents/violations for 3 years): 20%
- Defensive driving course: 10%
- Anti-theft device: 5%
- Good student (under 25, GPA 3.0+): 10%
- Multi-policy (home + auto): 12%
- Pay-in-full discount: 5%
- Paperless billing: 3%
- Low mileage (under 7,500 miles/year): 8%

SECTION 9: CLAIMS PROCESS

In the event of an accident:
1. Ensure safety and call emergency services if needed
2. Exchange information with other parties involved
3. Document the scene (photos, witness information)
4. Report the claim to the insurance company within 24 hours
5. Cooperate with the claims adjuster investigation
6. Obtain repair estimates from approved facilities
7. Do not authorize repairs until the adjuster has inspected

Claim settlement options:
- Repair: Vehicle repaired at an approved facility
- Actual Cash Value: Payment of fair market value minus deductible
- Total Loss: Vehicle value exceeds repair cost; ACV paid minus deductible
